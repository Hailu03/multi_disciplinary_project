My name is Le Thanh Hai or you can call me Hailu. Before my English name is Luke and I try to combine my Vietnamese name and English name into one name which is Hailuke. However, I think Hailu is shorter than previous name. Therefore, I choose it for my nickname.

A little more persistence, a little more effort, and what seemed hopeless failure may turn to glorious success.

Well, I was born in Vung Tau, a city surrounded by beautiful beaches. My mother and dad were born in the North of Vietnam. Because of that, most of my family members speak a northern accent. I grew up with my parents, they always took care of me and cared about my education. Until now, they still care about me. However, I'm mature enough to exceed my parents' envelopment. I have to stand by my side and have a stepping stone decision in all situations without them.

I love doing math and solving several hard exercises. Although it takes me long hours to reach a proper solution, I am still steady and calm down. Albert Einstein said that "Pure mathematics is, in its way, the poetry of logical ideas". I hopefully believe that I can fly high regardless any tough incidents happen in my life
